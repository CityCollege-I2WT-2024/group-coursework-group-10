Louis Armstrong's Biography

Born August 4th, 1901 in Louisiana, United States, and died on July 6th, 1971 in New York, United States. Louis Armstrong also known as "Satch" or "Pops" was an American Trumpeter, who was also regarded to as one of the best, and an artist with a very high impact in Jazz. in his career which spanned almost 50 years he was apart of multiple music groups one of them being "Louis Armstrong and His Hot Five". Louis Armstrong was and still is loved by many Jazz enjoyers as he changed the field of Jazz, and not only Jazz but music as a whole.
