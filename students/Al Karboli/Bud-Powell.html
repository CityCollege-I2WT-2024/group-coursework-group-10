Bud Powell's Biography

On September 27th, 1924 in Harlem, New York a musical Prodigy was born famously known as "Bud" Earl Rudolph Bud Powell emerged onto the scene during the middle of the 1900s Despite many challenges during his uprise Bud Powell managed to beat health problems, and daily personal struggles. He released many musical pieces which are timeless. Tracks like "Un Poco Loco", and "Bouncing With Bud" are just proof of how he managed to change the scence of bepop and Jazz, and how he was a innovater who has his name recorded in the history books of not only Bepop and Jazz but also music as a whole.

Bud Powelln's Latest Album

Album Name: "Ups 'n Downs"

Released In: 1965

Released shortly before his death, this was one of if not the most emotional albums in music history, because despite his knowledge of his declining health Bud Powell showed incredible reselience in this album through his music this album was his way of displaying his love, and impact on Jazz and Bepop it was also unfortunatley a sad goodbye to one of Jazz's greatest pioneer.
