Milestones in Electronic Music

  • 1970s: The birth of electronic music as a genre with the rise of synthesizers and artists like Kraftwerk.
  • 1980s: The emergence of house music in Chicago and techno in Detroit, shaping the dance music scene.
  • 1990s: The popularization of trance, drum and bass, and rave culture across Europe and the world.
  • 2000s: EDM (Electronic Dance Music) becomes mainstream with global festivals and artists like Tiesto and David Guetta.
  • 2010s: The rise of dubstep, trap, and experimental electronic music, broadening the genre's appeal.
