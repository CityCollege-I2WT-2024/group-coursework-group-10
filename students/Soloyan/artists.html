Legends of Hip-Hop

Eminem

Known for his intricate wordplay and rapid-fire delivery, Eminem is a global hip-hop icon.

Drake

A versatile artist blending rap, R&B, and pop, Drake is a defining voice of modern hip-hop.

Ice Cube

A pioneering force in gangsta rap and a founding member of N.W.A.

Lil Uzi Vert

Known for his eccentric style and melodic flows, Uzi is a leading figure in modern rap.

Tupac Shakur

A revolutionary figure, Tupac addressed social issues with powerful lyrics.

Kendrick Lamar

Known for his storytelling ability and social commentary in hip-hop.
